Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Life Admin ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Life Admin app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Financial data — bills, subscriptions, and expenses you manually enter
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (crash reports, feature usage)
  • Device information — device type and OS version for compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the Life Admin service
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Send bill reminders and alerts you have requested
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve app stability through crash reports

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never store your banking passwords or credentials.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your financial data with advertisers
  • We do not access your bank accounts directly
  • We do not store your payment card numbers

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic — AI assistant (privacy policy)
  • OneSignal — push notifications (privacy policy)

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for bill reminders and alerts. You can disable these at any time in your device Settings or within the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Life Admin is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time and will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
